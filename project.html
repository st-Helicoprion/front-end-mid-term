Project About Us

The Main Hook : The Sound Wave Mechanic

Use sound waves, created through your toys, to explore the pitch dark world around you. Different toys bring in new exciting ways to see in the dark, use them to the best of your abilities !

The Story and World

In a pitch dark world, play as a boy with toys that illuminate through sound waves. Find all the toys and use them to free your captured best friend. The more toys you collect, the stronger your bond and the further she is from despair!

Embark Now !

Your best friend has been captured ! Find powerful toys in complete darkness and reach her before she loses hope !
Oh we forgot to mention, you won't be alone in the dark, we've made sure to provide some devious company to keep you on your toes ! Bon Voyage~
